About me + Book recommendations

I'm a teen author, poet, and musician. I love coding, chess, and minecraft. Top three good books/series I've read recently were:

  • The Merciful Crow by Margaret Owen. A good story would recommend.
  • Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere. If you haven't already read this, you need to. It's a lot of books, but it's all amazing and worth reading.
  • The Prison Healer by Lynette Noni. This trilogy is a great read, although there was quite a bit of romance in the second book, which isn't my favorite. Still, I would definitely recommend.
